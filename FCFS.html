First Come First Serve

(FCFS)

First Come First Serve (FCFS) is the simplest of all Disk Scheduling Algorithms.

In FCFS, the requests are addressed in the order they arrive in the disk queue.

Suppose a disk has 200 tracks (0-199). The order of requests is: (82, 170, 43, 140, 24, 16, 190).

The current position of the Read/Write head is 50.
