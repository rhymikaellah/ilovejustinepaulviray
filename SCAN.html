Scan

In SCAN algorithm the disk arm moves into a particular direction and services the requests coming in its path and after reaching

the end of disk, it reverses its direction and again services the request arriving in its path.

So, this algorithm works as an elevator and hence also known as elevator algorithm.

Suppose a disk having 200 tracks (0-199). the order of request is in - (82,170,43,140,24,16,190)

The current position of Read/Write head is : 50, and it is also given that the disk arm should move “towards the larger value”
